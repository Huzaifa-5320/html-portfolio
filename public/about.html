About Me

Beyond academics, I actively seek opportunities to bridge theory and practice by participating in AI-focused workshops, hackathons, and research initiatives. These experiences not only expand my technical expertise but also cultivate skills like teamwork, creativity, and adaptability. As I continue my journey at Karachi University, I am committed to pushing the boundaries of my knowledge, embracing challenges, and contributing to the advancement of AI. My ultimate aspiration is to make meaningful contributions to the global tech community, leveraging AI to solve complex problems and improve lives.
